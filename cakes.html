ABOUT OUR CAKES

We are a boutique cake shop. I've had 30 years of experience developing my recipes. I continue to grow and learn everyday and I hope I never stop learning and stretching my talents. I seek to design and deliver a unique cake experience. I do not work in fondant, and I'm not a huge fan of writing on cakes. I look at my cakes as a canvas. Each cake is a work of art and at the same time I will be making one of the best tasting cakes you've ever had. Thank you for your support and in return you will be getting my very best!

Much Love,

Melissa and Patty
